Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly to track expenses, we store the data you enter: amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and powering the features you use. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorised access, including encrypted storage and secure connections (HTTPS).

You may request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics services that process your expense data.

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us through the contact information provided on the site.
